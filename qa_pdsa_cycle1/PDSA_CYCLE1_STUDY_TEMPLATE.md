# PDSA Cycle 1 Study Template

## User-Test Tasks

1. Start on Overview and choose the best route for planning a study.
2. Find where to validate source readiness and unresolved QA issues.
3. Open the MCL/TRIANGLE panel and explain whether it is causal evidence.
4. Identify whether a displayed TRIANGLE count is accepted production aggregate or fallback/reference.
5. Search for Ki-67, p53, PCD, CLL, and not in database.
6. Open the Data Dictionary and find the source, raw column, confidence, caveat, and export actions for a concept.
7. Open Quick Start and identify the recipe for a new employee building a diagnosis or drug-exposed cohort.

## Study Questions

- Can the reviewer explain what DALY-CARE can support now within 60 seconds?
- Can the reviewer tell fallback/reference values from accepted production aggregate values?
- Does the MCL/TRIANGLE panel read as feasibility-only and non-causal?
- Is the data-manager route findable without first reading dense source tables?
- Are disease routes and onboarding routes discoverable through both cards and search?

## Review Notes

- Record any term that a reviewer searches for but cannot find.
- Record any count or badge that feels like an unsupported production claim.
- Record mobile screenshots where the first meaningful content is pushed too far down or covered by controls.
- Do not record or paste patient-level values, raw text, raw dates, CPR values, or identifiers.
