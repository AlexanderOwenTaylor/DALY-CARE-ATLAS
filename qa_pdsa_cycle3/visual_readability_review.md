# Cycle 3 Visual Readability Review

## Review Targets

- Treatment ATC/SKS/SMR.
- DaMyDa imaging and bone disease.
- Microbiology organism, source, antibiotic, and susceptibility panels.
- Pathology SNOMED, specimen/material, institution/source, and free-text availability.
- Vitals as the positive reference pattern.
- MCL/TRIANGLE and CONFLUENCE feasibility panels.

## Pass Criteria

- Meaningful labels are visible without hover.
- Bars state whether they scale by count or percent.
- Raw/source labels are preserved in lineage, not used alone as primary headings.
- Free-text availability is status-first.
- Empty states do not precede populated evidence in the same panel section.
